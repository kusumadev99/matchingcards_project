Instructions:

  • Click the cards randomly,the timer also starts.

  • Intially your score is set to be 0.

  • If any two cards(first & second flipped cards)are found to be match then u got a point.

  • The cards(first flipped card & second flipped card)doesn't match then they remain unflipped until u get matched them with thier respective card.

  • if you match all the pairs within the given time limit then you won the game.

  • if you doesn't match all pairs in the given time limit then time is over and u need to restart the game.
